1-1 Coaching

A process, not a preset program.

Personalized and evolving - designed around your biology, your resources, and real life. Built on Stay Ahead™, mapped to Temeli.

01

Baseline assessmentData first

We start by understanding where you actually are - not how you feel, not what you assume. Depending on your profile: blood work, DNA testing, DEXA and body composition, VO2 max, movement and recovery indicators, stress load review. We don't build plans on guesses.

02

Resource analysisReality matters

No system works if it ignores reality. We map what you actually have: time, training and recovery capacity, budget, external constraints. This step determines what is sustainable - not what looks good on paper.

03

Plan 1Simple, focused, executable

Not the perfect plan - the right starting plan. Minimal enough to fit your life, structured enough to create signal, designed to stick, not impress. We start working immediately.

04

IterationUntil it sticks

Plans evolve only when behavior stabilizes. We adjust frequency, volume, intensity, recovery, and rituals - at a rhythm that matches your capacity. We don't change things before they have time to work.

05

MonitoringFeedback loops

Progress is tracked, not assumed: performance markers, recovery signals, subjective feedback, periodic reassessment. When relevant, we repeat key tests - not to chase numbers, but to confirm direction.

06

RecalibrationProgress compounds

At defined intervals we step back and reassess biology, capacity, constraints, and goals - then recalibrate the system. This is how progress compounds. Quietly. Over time.

FAQ

Read this before you fill the form. It might save us both time.

Do we meet in person?

No. All coaching is 100% remote, via Zoom or a similar platform, with follow-ups by message or email. It's effective, efficient, and built for real life.

What happens during a session?

We dive in fast. We clarify your why, assess your current state through tests, indicators, or reflection, and I give you tools to apply in real life. Once we find what works, we build it into a habit. You leave each session with clarity, pressure, and a decision - not fluff.

Do you work with beginners?

I work with people who are ready. You can be a complete beginner if you're serious. But if you want hand-holding, quick fixes, or hacks - I'm not your guy.

What kind of clients do you work with?

Entrepreneurs, high performers, overwhelmed parents, burned-out athletes, former warriors. People who want to own their habits, health, and headspace. If you're ready to build something real, we'll get along.

Do you write workout or meal plans?

No. I won't send you PDFs. I'll help you discover what works best for your biology, time, and goals - and help you build systems and habits that last.

Do you offer packages or memberships?

Most people start with a single session. Some move to the 6-session Transformation Package, valid for 300 days. You come back when you're ready - usually less often over time - until you don't need direction anymore. No pressure. No subscription. Just real progress.

What is unique about your coaching?

Cutting-edge science, tech, and more than anything - common sense. We can analyze your DNA, DEXA scan, or other data to personalize your approach, but we always start with the Temeli foundations: Breath, Sleep, Movement, Nutrition, Mindfulness, Prevention. We optimize behavior first, because that's what brings sustainable results.
